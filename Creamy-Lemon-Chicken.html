Creamy Lemon Chicken Recipe

Creamy lemon chicken pasta is a delightful and flavorful dish that combines the richness of creamy sauce with the zesty kick of lemon and the savory goodness of chicken. Here's a simple description of this delicious dish: Tender pieces of chicken breast are pan-seared to perfection, creating a golden brown exterior and a juicy, succulent interior. The chicken is then combined with al dente pasta, creating a perfect marriage of textures. The star of the dish is the luscious creamy lemon sauce. A velvety blend of heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and butter creates a decadent base. Freshly squeezed lemon juice is added to the sauce, infusing it with a bright and citrusy flavor that complements the richness of the cream. The pasta and chicken are generously coated in the creamy lemon sauce, ensuring each bite is a harmonious blend of creaminess and tanginess. To elevate the dish further, a sprinkle of chopped parsley adds a burst of freshness and a pop of color. The result is a mouthwatering dish that balances the richness of cream with the vibrant and zesty notes of lemon, creating a truly satisfying and indulgent culinary experience.

Ingredients

    4-6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
    1 teaspoon salt
    1 teaspoon ground black pepper
    8 tablespoons butter
    1 cup chicken broth
    2 lemons juice of the lemons
    2 lemons zest of lemons
    1 cup heavy cream
    1/2 tablespoon cornstarch
    1/2 box spaghetti

Instructions

    Heat a large skillet of medium-high heat. Add olive oil to pan.
    Season chicken breasts with Italian seasoning, salt and pepper. Place chicken breasts in a skillet and brown on both sides, about 4-6 minutes per side.
    Coat the slow cooker with cooking spray and add chicken breasts into the slow cooker.
    Add lemon juice, lemon zest, chicken broth and butter to the slow cooker and cook for 4 hours.
    Remove chicken and shred with a fork. Set aside. Stir together cornstarch and cream.
    Pour the cream mixture in a slow cooker and the sauce cook for about 10 minutes.
    While the sauce is thickening, cook spaghetti noodles according to the package.
    Place shredded chicken and pasta into the slow cooker. Toss together before serving. Top with parsley and lemon slices for garnish.
