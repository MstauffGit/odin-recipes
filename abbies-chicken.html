Abbie's Chicken recipe

Ingredients

    1/2 lb skinless Chicken Breast
    1 Can Corn
    1 can Black Beans - rinsed
    1 can Rotel
    Pack Regular Cream Cheese 16oz
    1 pack Ranch Dressing mix

Instructions

    Add chicken to slow cooker
    add Corn (juice, to) and Black Beans (rinsed)
    add Rotel
    add Cream cheese and Ranch Dressing mix
    Cover: Low - 6 hrs or High - 3hrs

Let cool slightly before serving
